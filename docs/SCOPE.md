# Aurelius — Scope & ROI

> The visual version lives at `docs/SCOPE.html` (open in a browser). This is the plain-text record.

## What it is
A private, operator-class personal OS — not a chatbot, not an app. It runs your day in the
background, reads what you can't, remembers what you shouldn't have to, and gets sharper about
you every week it's alive. Local-first and sovereign: it lives on your Mac Mini and your drive,
not someone's cloud.

## A day, run in parallel (all built)
- **06:30 macro** — market pulse: fed funds, 10-yr, yield curve, CPI, mortgage rates + your topics → filed to the brain.
- **07:00 briefing** — pushed to your phone in your voice: focus, overdue, where the deep-work block goes.
- **daytime capture** — voice notes transcribe and file themselves, tagged, surfaced when relevant.
- **13:00 nudge** — silent when on pace; corrective when you've drifted.
- **21:30 debrief** — what closed, what slipped, the honest intent-vs-action gap.
- **Sunday** — the whole week unattended: research sweep, wiki rewrite, persona observation, planning, scoreboard.

## Seven systems (live, verified)
1. **Second Brain** — four-write ingestion, semantic recall, living wiki, Obsidian mirror.
2. **Calendar & Planner** — 15-min Google sync, availability, overload math, weekly session.
3. **Financial Edge** — FRED macro + daily market pulse, steerable topics.
4. **Inbox Hands** — Gmail read + draft-only (no send scope by construction).
5. **Missions Engine** — plan → execute → report → ingest.
6. **Learning Loop** — propose→confirm, compiled reuse, persona observer, corrections.
7. **Scoreboard** — operator score + LLM-dependence rate, weekly.

## The $3,000 ROI
- **Not a sunk cost for one app** — Mini + NAS are general infra (backups, storage, compute) for a decade. One-time ~$3,000 ≈ **$50/month over 5 years**, then free.
- **Replaces:** a SaaS stack ($60–150/mo rented forever), stands in for the load of a VA ($1–2k/mo). Only ongoing cost is API credits ($20–50/mo, designed to fall over time).
- **Return:** ~5 hrs/week back; more athletes served per coaching hour (training engine writes feedback+PRs); a daily financial edge; and an asset that appreciates because it learns you.
- **Break-even: 2–4 months.** Caveat, said straight: the ROI is conditional on actually living in it. It multiplies effort you're already putting in; it isn't magic.
